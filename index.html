Utility Connection Steps & Costs

To connect your new home to city/county utilities, here are the general steps and associated costs:

  1. Verify Utility Availability: Contact the local city/county utility department to verify water, sewer, and electricity availability.
    Cost: $0 - $50
  2. Submit Application for Utility Connections: Fill out necessary applications for water, sewer, and electrical service.
    Cost: $100 - $500
  3. Sewer Connection Fees: Pay for the tap fee and infrastructure to connect to the city sewer.
    Cost: $3,000 - $6,000
  4. Water Connection Fees: Pay for the water tap fee and any required inspections.
    Cost: $1,000 - $3,500
  5. Electricity Connection: Costs for meter installation and service line.
    Cost: $500 - $2,500
  6. Gas Connection (if applicable): Costs for natural gas line installation.
    Cost: $500 - $2,000
  7. Permit Fees: Building permits for construction and utility connections.
    Cost: $500 - $2,000
  8. Site Preparation Costs: Minor grading and trenching for utilities.
    Cost: $5,000 - $10,000

Anticipated Project Timelines for Calvert County

Below is the general timeline for project approvals and construction based on Calvert County's requirements:

  1. Initial Land Preparation & Permits: 1-2 months
    • Soil testing, site surveys, and grading
    • Building permit approval (usually within 3-4 weeks)
  2. Home Manufacturing: 2-3 months
    • Depending on the selected modular home, the manufacturing process can take approximately 8-12 weeks.
  3. Delivery & Setup: 1-2 weeks
    • Home delivery to the site
    • Modular home assembly and foundation setup
  4. Utility Connection & Final Inspections: 1 month
    • Water, sewer, and electricity connections
    • Final inspections from Calvert County
  5. Total Estimated Time: 5-7 months

Note: These timelines are subject to change based on the complexity of the project and local government approval times.
